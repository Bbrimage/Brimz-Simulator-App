01

Who We Are

Brimz ("Brimz," "we," "our," or "us") operates a fan engagement platform used at live sports and entertainment events. The Brimz platform includes a mobile app (iOS and Android), a smart wristband worn at events, and web-based dashboards used by venue operators.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the Brimz mobile app or wristband. It applies to all fans who download and use the Brimz app.

02

Data We Collect

Account Information

When you create a Brimz account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Username (optional)
  • Profile photo (optional)

Engagement Signal Data

When you wear the Brimz wristband at an event, we collect:

  • Motion data — physical movement, detected via accelerometer (MPU6050)
  • Audio response — crowd sound level near the wristband, used to measure cheer response
  • Heart rate — beats per minute, measured via optical sensor (MAX30102)

Important: These signals are collected exclusively to calculate your Fan Energy Score during the event. They are engagement measurements — not health or medical data. Heart rate is stored as elevation above your personal baseline established at pairing, not as an absolute medical measurement.

Usage Data

  • Event attendance history
  • Challenges completed
  • Rewards earned and redeemed
  • Badges unlocked
  • In-app interactions and screen views

Device & Technical Data

  • Device identifiers
  • Bluetooth identifiers (for wristband pairing)
  • Operating system and app version
  • Push notification token (if you enable notifications)

Location Data

We do not collect precise GPS location. We may infer a general venue location from the event you are attending.

04

Engagement Signals & Biometric Data

The Brimz wristband collects motion, audio, and heart rate signals. We take the following precautions with this data:

  • All signal data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS — never in plain text
  • Data is encrypted at rest on our servers
  • Heart rate is stored as elevation above your personal baseline — your resting heart rate is not retained after your session ends
  • Raw sensor data is never shared with third parties
  • Signal data is not used for health screening, insurance, or employment purposes
  • Signal data is not integrated with Apple HealthKit, Google Health Connect, or any health platform
  • Your explicit consent is required before any signal collection begins

You are in control. You can withdraw your consent to biometric and audio data collection at any time from the Settings screen in the app. Withdrawal stops all future collection but does not affect data already collected unless you request deletion.

05

Third-Party Sharing

Venue Operators

The venue hosting your event can see aggregated crowd energy data and participation metrics. Venue operators cannot see your individual biometric readings — only your Fan Energy Score, ranking position, and challenge participation status.

Service Providers

We use the following third-party services to operate the platform:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and backend infrastructure
  • Expo / EAS — mobile app build and update delivery
  • Apple APNs / Google FCM — push notification delivery (if you enable notifications)

These providers access your data only as necessary to provide their services to us, under data processing agreements consistent with this policy.

What We Never Do

  • Sell your personal data to any third party
  • Share raw biometric/sensor readings outside of Brimz infrastructure
  • Use your data for targeted advertising
  • Share data with employers, insurers, or government entities (except as required by law)
06

Partner API Access

Sports teams and venue partners may connect to the Brimz platform via our API to display fan engagement data within their own apps or in-arena experiences.

Your data is never shared with a partner without your explicit consent. Before any partner can access your data:

  • You will be shown a consent screen explaining exactly what data the partner will receive and why
  • You can grant access to one partner without granting access to all partners
  • Partners only receive the specific data scopes you approve
  • You can revoke a partner's access at any time from Settings → Privacy & Data

All partners must sign a Partner Agreement committing to data protection standards equivalent to our own before receiving API access. Partners cannot re-share or sell your data.

Every partner data access request is logged in our audit system with a timestamp, the data accessed, and the partner's identity.

Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability — Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw Consent — Withdraw consent to biometric/audio collection at any time
  • Revoke Partner Access — Revoke any partner's access to your data at any time
  • Opt Out of Notifications — Disable push notifications at any time in Settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@brimz.tech or use the in-app controls in Settings → Privacy & Data.

09

Data Deletion

You can delete your Brimz account and all associated data at any time:

  • In the app: Settings → Privacy & Data → Delete My Data
  • Online form: brimz.tech/delete — available even if you have uninstalled the app
  • By email: privacy@brimz.tech

Deletion removes your account record, engagement history, earned badges, token balance, and all associated session data. Some data may be retained for up to 30 days in backup systems before permanent removal. API audit logs are retained for a minimum of 12 months for compliance purposes, with your personal identifiers anonymized.

Note: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Your earned badges, event history, and token balance cannot be recovered after deletion.

10

Children's Privacy

The Brimz app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@brimz.tech and we will delete the information promptly.

Users between the ages of 13 and 17 should have a parent or guardian review this Privacy Policy. Biometric data collection from users under 18 requires verifiable parental consent where required by applicable law.

11

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Data is encrypted at rest in our database
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely using platform Keychain (iOS) and Keystore (Android)
  • Row-level security policies ensure fans can only access their own data
  • Partner API keys are stored as cryptographic hashes — plain-text keys are never stored
  • Voucher codes are generated server-side and expire after 15 minutes
  • Access to production data is limited to authorized Brimz personnel

No security system is impenetrable. If you discover a vulnerability, please report it to security@brimz.tech.
