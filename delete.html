Account Deletion

Delete My Data

Request permanent deletion of your Brimz account and all associated personal data. This page is available even if you've uninstalled the app.

What gets deleted
  • Your account, email address, and profile information
  • Full event attendance history and Fan Energy Scores
  • All earned badges and token balance
  • Wristband session data and raw engagement signals
  • Reward redemption history and voucher records
  • All partner data access consents
  • Push notification preferences
What's retained for compliance

Partner API audit logs are retained for a minimum of 12 months with your personal identifiers anonymized. Backup systems may retain your data for up to 30 days before permanent removal.

!

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Your badges, event history, and token balance cannot be recovered after deletion is processed.
